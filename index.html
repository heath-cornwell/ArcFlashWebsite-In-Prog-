ARC FLASH

  Arc Flash is a dynamic local band known for their electrifying performances and deep connection to the community. Recently, they have taken part in a battle of the bands fundraiser, aiming to support Children's National Hospital. Their music, which blends various genres, resonates with a diverse audience and showcases their versatility and passion. Competing in this event highlights their commitment to using their talents for a noble cause, as they strive to bring attention and resources to the hospital's important work. Arc Flash's involvement in the fundraiser underscores their dedication not only to their art but also to making a positive impact in the lives of children and families in need.
